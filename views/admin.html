Employee login — default password▾

Bulk import▾

One employee per line as Associate ID, Name, Business Unit, Team, Campus (comma or tab separated). Business Unit, Team and Campus are optional. A header row is ignored — you can paste straight from your sheet.

Campus locations (geofencing)▾

Set each campus's coordinates and an allowed radius. Employees whose campus has coordinates can only check in within that radius. Leave a campus blank to disable geofencing for it. Tip: on Google Maps, right-click a spot → click the lat,lng to copy.
